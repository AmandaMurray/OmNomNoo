Where should you be eating?

Your diet has a major impact on the planet. Both food production and transportation can cause massive amounts of carbon emissions, worsening climate change.

Om Nom NOOO helps students make educated decisions about where they eat. It provides scores for different restaurants based on their carbon footprint. By making the green choice easy, we encourage people to be mindful of what they eat, and to think about how it impacts the planet.
